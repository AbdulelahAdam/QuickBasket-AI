About QuickBasket AI

Our Mission

At QuickBasket AI, our mission is to empower smart shoppers with intelligent tools to make better purchasing decisions. We believe everyone deserves access to real-time price information and market insights to save money and time.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, QuickBasket AI was created to solve a real problem: tracking prices manually across multiple marketplaces is tedious and ineffective. We developed a browser extension that automatically monitors prices, identifies trends, and alerts users when prices drop below their target.

What We Do

QuickBasket AI provides a comprehensive price tracking solution that:

  • Monitors prices in real-time across major e-commerce platforms
  • Uses AI algorithms to predict price drops and recommend optimal purchase times
  • Sends smart alerts when prices match your target
  • Provides visual price history and trend analysis
  • Saves users significant amounts on every purchase

Our Values

  • Transparency: We believe in being honest about our features and pricing
  • Privacy: Your data is yours - we never sell or share personal information
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our AI to provide better insights
  • Customer Focus: Everything we do is designed with the user in mind

Looking Forward

We're constantly expanding our marketplace coverage and adding new features based on user feedback. Our roadmap includes support for more e-commerce platforms, advanced analytics dashboards, and team collaboration features.
